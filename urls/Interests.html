Brooks Falls

The bears of Kodiak Alaska are some of the most iconic brown bears in the United States. They will wait at Brooks Falls in Katmai National Park in Alaska and catch salmon as the fish try to swim up the falls to their spawning grounds. There is a video camera set up so that many people can see this event without having to travel to the falls, which are only accessible by plane, and disrupt the bears.
