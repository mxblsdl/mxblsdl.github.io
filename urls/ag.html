C-BREC Agricultural Modeling Tool

  • question_answerInstructions
    Agricultural residue base estimates were derived from earlier work by the California Biomass Collaborative. We focus our analysis on the eight crops that dominate agricultural residue production in California: rice, grapes, almonds, corn, wheat, cotton, and walnuts. Together, these eight crops comprise over 80% of the estimated technically available annual mass of residues in the state. This project does not consider food processing residues as it is assumed the majority of these residues have existing market pathways
